The Legal Threshold: Can You Apply?

Many parents are told they need a formal diagnosis or "years of evidence" to apply for an assessment. This is a myth.

Under Section 36(8) of the Children and Families Act 2014, the Local Authority must carry out an EHC needs assessment if:

  • The child has or may have special educational needs (SEN); and
  • It may be necessary for special educational provision to be made in accordance with an EHC plan.

Crucial Note: The law uses the word "MAY." This is a intentionally low legal bar. You do not need to prove an EHCP is definitely required yet—only that it might be.
